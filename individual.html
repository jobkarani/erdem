Motor Private/Commercial Insurance

Covers loss or damage to the vehicle including legal liability to third parties in respect of death, injury or property damage arising from the use of the motor vehicle. Motor private covers vehicles that are used for private/domestic purposes while motor commercial covers vehicles used for business/commercial purposes.

Medical Insurance

This covers medical expenses of the insured and their dependents. The common covers comprise out-patient and in-patient covers with dental and optical extensions.

Aviation Insurance

This is geared specifically to the operation of aircraft and the risks involved in aviation.

Group Personal Accident Cover

This insurance provides compensation to your employees in the event of injuries, disability or death caused solely by accidental, external and visible events.

Marine Insurance

Covers the loss or damage of ships, cargo, terminals, and any transport by which the property is transferred, acquired, or held between the points of origin and the final destination.

Education and Investment Policy

The smartest way to secure your child's future begins with investing in the right education plan. Our Career Life Plus cover is tailored to ensure your child's dreams are fulfilled through a well-managed fund. Start saving today and we will help your child realise their aspirations.

Claim Management

Erdem Insurance Agency provides our clients a swift
process in making claims and manages making of the claims.

Theft Insurance

This covers loss or damage caused by the unlawful taking of property. Theft insurance as a classification includes Burglary insurance, money or cash in transit etc.

Group Life Cover

Group Life Insurance comprises group life insurance schemes mainly organized by employers on behalf of their employees.

Engineering Insurance

This offers protection against a variety of risks associated with erection, resting and working machinery, plant or equipment. Examples are contractors all risks, Erection all risks, machinery breakdown, electronic equipment, deterioration of stock etc.

Professional Indemnity Policy

This is a liability cover designed to protect professionals, business owners, and the self-employed if clients claim to have incurred a loss due to negligence of the professional.

Funeral Insurance

This covers funeral expenses upon demise of the insured or their dependents. It can be purchased as a stand alone product or as part of another insurance product such as life insurance or medical insurance. It is renewed every 12 months.

Work Injury Benefits Insurance (WIBA)

This provides compensation to employees for work-related injuries and diseases contracted in the course of employment.

Pension/Retirement Plans

We mainly offere this to help individuals build up a sum of money that can be used in retirement. Pension plans can also be taken by groups such as employers. We invest the money to generate a regular income paid to the retiree and this income is what is referred to as pension.

Annuity

This is another way of planning for retirement. In an annuity, an individual pays a lump sum premium to us. We then provide a lifetime income to the individual from their premium.

Home (Fire Domestic) Insurance

This covers your house (building) and belongings (content) against risks including; fire, theft, damage by extreme weather and other losses. The insurance can also be extended to cover domestic workers, occupiers and owners liabilities.

Fire Industrial Insurance

This provides cover against loss or damage to property caused by fire, lightning and explosion. This insurance covers assets such as buildings, plant and machinery, stock insurance, furniture fixtures and fittings and office equipment.

Miscellaneous Insurance

This includes other types of insurance not covered in the above classes such as Agriculture, Golfers, Travel, Bonds, Plate Glass etc.
